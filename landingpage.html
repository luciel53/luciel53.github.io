About us

"Passionate about breeding and particularly Cat breeding, I (Lucie) tried to make my own breeding but unfortunately it didn't work. So I asked to myself: How can I stay in this environment without make breeding ? I realized it was difficult to find a stud cat to make a good selection. So I decided to create 2 groups on Facebook to help contacts between breeders. But Facebook has its disadvantages. The page doesn't seem very professional, offers are messy. And the management of the breeders is very complicated. So an application can be a good alternative. Murdo partnered with me to realize this project."

Lucie Leroty
